Privacy Policy for Air Ally

Air Ally is developed and operated by AramisALLY (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how Air Ally collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Air Ally mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Used only to provide real-time airport navigation, gate directions, AR walking paths, accessibility routes, and relevant airport information. Location data is processed in real time and is not sold or shared.
  • Camera Access (AR Navigation): Camera access is used solely to enable augmented reality navigation overlays (such as directional arrows and walking paths) inside airports. Camera data is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Analytics & Crash Data: We collect anonymized analytics and crash reports to improve app stability, performance, and user experience. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Device & Usage Information: Basic technical data such as device type, operating system version, and app interaction metrics may be collected for diagnostics and optimization.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate airport maps, gate locations, and terminal navigation
  • Deliver real-time updates for gate changes and airport layout changes
  • Enable AR navigation and accessibility-friendly routing
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and features
  • Detect and fix bugs or crashes

Accessibility & Language Support

Air Ally is designed to support accessible navigation routes and may adapt navigation instructions for mobility-friendly paths where available. Language and accessibility features are continuously improved to enhance usability for all travelers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Anonymized analytics data may be processed by trusted service providers strictly for app performance and crash diagnostics.

Data Retention

We retain only anonymized and aggregated data for as long as necessary to improve the app. Location and camera data are not stored.

Security

We use standard industry security measures to protect data processed by the app. All communication uses secure system-level encryption provided by the operating system.

Children’s Privacy

Air Ally is rated for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Your Choices

You may control or disable location and camera permissions at any time through your device settings. Some features may not function without these permissions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
